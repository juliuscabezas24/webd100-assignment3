WHO ARE THE NOMADS?

The Nomads are a R&B band composed of the dynamic duo of Alex Carrington and Nate Sullivan, who both have been immersed in the world of music since they could stand on their own two feet. Growing up together in the same elementary school, a friendship between the two took root through a bundle of energy, that they expressed through music. By their twenties they kick-started their first band, where they composed their own songs day by day. Today, Alex and Nate have spent dozens of nights demoing and designing hundreds of songs, learning and practicing to the point where music is now second nature to them. Now, they present There very own, first studio album, "Out of Sync"


OUT OF SYNC - The Nomads

Click to Preview the Album

"OUT OF SYNC" aims to hit on a key message. It's okay to be lost. The album plays on themes of dissociation and distortion, and the feeling of not knowing what you want to be. That was the case with Alex and Nate, they never knew this is where they would be a decade later after meeting. So, be OUT OF SYNC, its okay.
